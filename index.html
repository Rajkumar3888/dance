About Me

Namaste! I am a dedicated Bharatanatyam classical dancer, passionate about preserving and spreading the beauty of this ancient Indian art form. Trained under the guidance of my esteemed Guru NANI master , I have been performing Bharatanatyam since a young age, and over the years, it has become more than just a dance—it is my spiritual expression and life’s calling. My dance journey is rooted in tradition but open to innovation, allowing me to perform both pure classical margams and contemporary choreographies that connect with today’s audience. Through abhinaya (expressions), nritta (rhythmic movements), and bhava (emotions), I bring stories from mythology, history, and daily life to the stage. I have had the honor of performing at prestigious stages and festivals, receiving recognition and awards for my dedication and artistry. Each performance is an offering—a prayer in motion..
